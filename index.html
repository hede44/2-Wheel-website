Two Wheels Project

Turkey Tour - 3000KM
(Trabzon-Erbil)

in 2021 two of our Cyclers had a long tour to turkey, they passed by more than 400 villages and 41 cities, stayed for 29 days in Turkey and 2 days in Kurdistan Region of Iraq, the start point was in Uzungul lake-Trabzon - Northern part of Turkey, the journey in ground route way near to the black sea took 12 days until they reached Istanbul, after 6 days they reached Antalya Western part of Turkey the direction changed to Mediterranean Sea, after 5 days they reached Gazi Antap city, after 6 days they reached Slopi-last point of Turkey, and after 2 days they reached Erbil-capital of Kurdistan Region and the reception Ceremony was held with the attendance of the governor of Erbil and some other government officials.

Our project

Two wheels project is showing the power of Kurdish young people in bicycle field by doing long Distance Cycling tours and travels. And it is for the first time that we have such a project in Kurdistan region. Our Target in this project is to motivate our society to use bicycle instead of other types of transportation methods, and for taking care of health and environment.

Internationalizing Two wheels

Two Wheels is working to let the local athletes and international athletes get closer and meet each other, and to affect them for having new sports projects with each other, our aim in this work is to make connections between local and international athletes to improve sport’s sector and cycling.

Background of Two Wheels

As a group of young people, we work on this project on local and international levels in a modern and different way, despite the continuous obstacles ahead of the project.
